My (Super Duper) Definitive Top 3 Artists






3. Lemon Demon


Starting his music and video career online, Neil Cicierega has made 5 albums so far. He's an indie artist (A common theme here) who makes weird and zany songs following an album theme. They all have a groovy feeling, simillar to older songs.

Touch Tone Telephone, Spirit Phone


2. Tally Hall


Composed of band members Rob Cantor, Joe Hawley, Ross Federman, Andrew Horowitz, and Zubin Sedghi they made
indie music and skits before going on an indefinite hiatus (Though they still independently make music).
They're known for not being exactly normal (often silly), while still sounding great, having emotion, and executing their own style.

Good Day, Marvin's Marvelous Mechanical Museum


1. Toby "Radiation" Fox


Most well known for creating hit games Undertale and Deltarune , he mostly composes all the tracks in said games.
He has a history of composing for Homestuck since 2009, and all of his songs have a certain charm that just can't be replicated.
It's because of this that his songs have been stuck in my head for over 10 years (even if 6 year old me couldnt afford his games).

"Roulxs Kaard Battle Theme - Piano Version"
